🕵️‍♀️ Steganography Toy

Steganography is the art of hiding secret messages inside seemingly ordinary files — like images or audio — so no one even knows a message is there.

In this tool, your secret text is concealed inside the pixels of an image using a method called Least Significant Bit (LSB) encoding. It’s like writing in invisible ink, but in digital form!

Use it to secretly share a password, code, or surprise message. But remember: anyone who knows about steganography can try to extract it — so use responsibly!

💡 Only someone with this tool can decode the message. Keep it secret, keep it safe!
