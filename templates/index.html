Shieldy

Light Tools. Strong Shield.

Cybersecurity doesn't have to be scary. Shieldy is a simple toolkit that helps you stay safe online — no jargon, no fuss.

With just a few clicks, you can:

  • 🔗 Spot Suspicious Links: Check if a URL looks sketchy or dangerous before clicking.
  • 🛡️ Test Your Privacy: Take the “Am I Oversharing?” quiz and see how much info you’re unknowingly giving away.
  • 🕵️‍♀️ Hide Messages in Images: Try the Steganography Toy — a fun way to embed secret text into image files, like digital invisible ink.

Whether you're a student, new to the web, or just curious — Shieldy gives you the tools to browse smarter, not harder.
